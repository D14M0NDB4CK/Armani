### The all-in-one Discord Managment bot for moderation, tickets, leveling, logging, integrations, and more!


## Features

#### Twitch & Youtube Integrations ~ *Coming Soon*
* Send live notifications straight to your discord server 
* Send Notifications to discord when a new video/vod is posted
* Get notifications when your favorite streamers go live
* Twitch & Youtube Live Chat integrations

#### Ticketing System ~ *Coming Soon*
* Works directly in a Discord text chat
* Customizable ticket form 
* Completed tickets can be archived or deleted

#### Community Tools ~ *Coming Soon*
* Fully customizable **leveling system** with automatic role assignment 
* Fun minigames (ie. rock, paper, scisors & more)

## Plans for the future
