## Basic Commands

    **/ping**  : Checks Armani Bots latency to your server in how long it takes Armani to respond.  
    **/hello** : Say hello to Armani Bot, response will be a hello reply with your username.  

## Fun Games

    **/rps**      : Play rock, paper, scissors with Armani. Had random outcome.  
    **/coinflip** : Flips a coin, Heads or Tails.  
    **/roll**     : Default rolls a 6 sided die. Has option for other dies (ex. 20 for a D20).  
    **/8ball**    : eightball fortune teller, ask it a question and see what it says.
