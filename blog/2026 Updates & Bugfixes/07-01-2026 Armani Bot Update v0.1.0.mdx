### First Update Official v0.1.0 ~ Not Public Yet 

We are excited to announce that we finally got Armani Bot to play mini games within discord!

This introduces the start of our humble little bot. This is not public yet as we have no current way to host Armani Bot, but we are working on it!

## 🎲 New Mini-Games

The following games are now available:

### 🪙 Coin Flip

Command: `/coinflip`

Feeling lucky? Flip a coin and let Armani Bot decide your fate.

### 🎲 Dice Roll

Command: `/roll + #of sides`

Roll the dice for a random number and use it for games, decisions, or just for fun. Will use multiple sides as well (ex. D20)

### ✂️ Rock, Paper, Scissors

Command: `/rps`

Challenge Armani Bot to a classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors and see if you can come out on top!

### 🎱 8-ball

Command: `/8ball`

Ask it a questions and see if what the outcome will be!
