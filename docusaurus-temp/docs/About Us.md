# About Us

We are but a small group of two with a big goal of providing a relief to streamers trying to make it to the big leagues. Whether it's improving your discord server, building a community, or wanting to streamline things instead of having several bots in your server at once, all doing different things. Our main goal is to streamline the process and give the community a bot that can do it all, and not charge out the bot for it! 🤣
